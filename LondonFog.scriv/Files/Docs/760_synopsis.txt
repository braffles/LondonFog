Henry Charles Ocelot, Marianne Hatton, and Edward Steen search Trafalgar Square for any sign of Resurrectionists. They find one, but they’re too late. It is revealed that Ocelot and Hatton were the subjects of Dr. Allen’s experiments, and that they have unusual powers - Mrs. Hatton pyrokinesis, HC Ocelot shapeshifting.

Henry Charles shapeshifts and eats the Resurrectionist, but the unliving are already spreading through the Square.
