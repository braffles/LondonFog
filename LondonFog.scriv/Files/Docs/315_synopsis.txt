Abraham Palmer helps Annabel to escape, though she mistrusts him. The Resurrectionists are hot on their heels. Abraham tells Annabel that the Resurrectionists believe she’s immune to Lazarus. He promises to explain everything when they arrive at Mr. Grimmer’s.

The Moirae bind and gag Roderick and take him for a ride in their carriage. Then they let him go and drive off. Roderick sends a message to Inspector Taggert via Rémy and returns to Connor’s mansion in pursuit of further clues. He discovers a connection between Connor and Varney the Vampire and a note in Connor’s edition of The Horrors of Allen’s Street.
